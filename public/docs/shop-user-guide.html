2. Getting Started

Accessing the Shop

To access the Quick Baskets online shop, simply navigate to:

Shop URL: leteres.com/shop

Creating an Account

While browsing is available to everyone, you'll need an account to place orders.

1 Click "Sign In" in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
2 Select "Create Account" if you're a new customer.
3 Fill in your details: Name, email address, phone number, and password.
4 Verify your email to activate your account.

3. Browsing Products

The Products Page

The Products page displays all available items in a clean, card-based layout. Each product card shows:

  • Product Image - Visual representation of the item
  • Product Name - Clear identification of the product
  • Barcode/SKU - For reference purposes
  • Price - Displayed in Philippine Peso (₱)
  • Add to Cart Button - Quick add functionality

Searching for Products

Use the search bar at the top of the products page to find specific items:

1 Enter the product name or barcode in the search field.
2 Click the search button or press Enter.
3 Results will update automatically to show matching products.
💡 Pro Tip

You can search by partial product names. For example, searching "milk" will show all milk-related products.

4. Using Categories

Navigation Menu Categories

Categories are accessible directly from the navigation bar for quick access:

On Desktop:

  • Hover over or click the "Categories" menu item
  • A dropdown will appear showing all available categories
  • Each category displays its icon and name
  • Click any category to filter products

On Mobile:

  • Tap the menu icon (☰) to open the navigation drawer
  • Tap "Categories" to expand the list
  • Scroll through categories and tap to select

Category Filter Indicator

When browsing a specific category, a filter label appears below the search bar showing the active category. Click the "X" button to clear the filter and view all products.

📂 Sample Categories

Rice, Canned Goods, Beverages, Snacks, Dairy, Frozen Foods, Cleaning Supplies, Personal Care, and more...

5. Shopping Cart

Adding Items to Cart

Adding products to your cart is simple and intuitive:

1 Find your product by browsing or searching.
2 Click the cart icon on the product card.
3 The item is added and the cart badge updates to show the count.

Viewing Your Cart

Click the cart icon in the navigation bar to view your shopping cart. The cart page displays:

Element Description
Product List All items you've added with images and details
Quantity Controls Increase or decrease item quantities
Item Subtotal Price × quantity for each item
Remove Button Delete items from your cart
Cart Total Sum of all items in your cart

Cart Badge

The cart icon in the header displays a badge showing the number of items in your cart. This updates in real-time as you add or remove products.

6. Customer Account

Account Dashboard

After signing in, access your dashboard by clicking your name in the navigation bar and selecting "Dashboard". Your dashboard provides:

  • Account Overview - Your profile information at a glance
  • Recent Orders - Quick view of your latest purchases
  • Quick Actions - Links to common tasks

User Menu Options

Click your name in the navigation to access:

Option Description
Dashboard Your account overview and quick stats
My Orders View all your order history and status
Logout Sign out of your account

7. Order Management

Placing an Order

Once you're ready to checkout:

1 Review your cart - Ensure all items and quantities are correct.
2 Click "Proceed to Checkout" to begin the checkout process.
3 Select pickup location - Choose your preferred store.
4 Confirm your order - Review the final details and submit.

Order Status

Track your order through the following stages:

Status Description
Pending Order received, awaiting confirmation
Verified Order confirmed and being prepared
Ready Order ready for pickup at the store
Completed Order has been picked up
Cancelled Order was cancelled

Viewing Order History

Access your complete order history from the "My Orders" page. Each order shows:

  • Order number and date
  • Items purchased
  • Total amount
  • Current status
  • Pickup location
